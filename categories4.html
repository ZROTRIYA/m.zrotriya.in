  • Accommodation will be provided from 31st march evening to 2nd April evening. All the participants coming before can also stay in and outside of K L University.

    All the students who need accommodation can either register online or after you have reached the college at the registration desk present at the entrance of the university.

    (Pre booking in advance is the best choice.)

    With the registration id, you can request the accommodation at hospitality desk which you will get directed by the members of registration desk.

    All participants are required to carry their original college identity cards with photo proof to avail the accommodation in the campus and are required to follow the Rules and Regulations as mentioned below.
